Cool, but how?

How do we do it?

In Person

In random selected venues, you get a conversation starting question to get the juices flowing, and seven minutes to get to know each other better. Studies show that 60% of people meet again after the event. We have several questions prepared that you might use or simply be yourself and make your own. Speeddating's friendly and fun twin sister, where the goal is to friendzone each other through engaging and good conversations.

Online

In our online events, one of two can happen:
You get access to a virtual world (aka the mother of the metaverse), our very own friendship world, where you have several shared rooms, spaces, games, and you get to meet people in a funny interactive speed friending way.
You have access to a rotating 1-to-1 videocall, maximazing the time and the number of people you will meet in short time slots.

How to participate?

We love feedback. Fill out the form below and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.

1 - Start by joining our meetup group and/or like our facebook page, telegram and whatsapp for further events announcements

2 - Join an event, RSVP on time and buy a ticket/reserve a seat on our eventfrog as described in each event.

3 - Show up in the location, be on time and enjoy!
